Old Royal Naval College

An architectural masterpiece sets on the banks of the River Thames in Greenwich. Its stunning baroque buildings and the breathtaking Painted Hall make it a remarkable destination for history enthusiasts, offering a glimpse into Britain’s naval past and royal heritage.
