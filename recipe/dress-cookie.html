Dress Cookie Recipe

A fun and delicious treat, these dress-shaped cookies are perfect for any occasion. With a buttery base and customizable chocolate decorations, they bring creativity and sweetness together in every bite!

Category : Cookie

Yield : ~70 pieces

Total Time : 2 hours

Date Created : 01/09/2024

❮ ❯

What to do

  • Soften the Butter : Take the butter out of the fridge and let it soften at room temperature
  • Mix Butter and Sugar : Add the caster sugar to the softened butter in batches, mixing well after each addition.
  • Incorporate the Egg : Add the egg to the butter-sugar mixture and mix until fully combined and no longer visible
  • Add Flour : Gradually add the flour to the mixture, sifting it in batches, and mix well.
  • Form and Freeze the Dough : After mixing everything together, roll the dough into a large, flat sheet, and freeze it.
  • Cut the Dough : Use a dress-shaped cookie cutter to cut the dough into shapes.
  • Bake the Cookies: Bake the cookies at 150°C (302°F) for 15 minutes. (You can add chocolate to the dough for additional variations.)
  • Decorate with Chocolate : Allow the cookies to cool completely, then dip them in melted chocolate to create your desired patterns.
