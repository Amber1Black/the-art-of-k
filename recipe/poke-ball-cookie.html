Poké Ball Cookie

Poké Ball Cookies are delightful treats shaped just like a Pokémon Poké Ball, making them irresistibly cute and fun! RECIPE

Poké Ball Cookie

Poké Ball Cookies are delightful treats shaped just like a Pokémon Poké Ball, making them irresistibly cute and fun! These cookies require a bit of time and effort to create, but the end result is totally worth it. With their vibrant colors and charming design, they are sure to bring a smile to anyone’s face - perfect for Pokémon fans of all ages.

Category : Cookie

Yield : ~50 pieces

Total Time : 3 hours

Date Created : 03/09/2024

❮ ❯

What you need

Butter

240g

Caster Sugar

80g

Egg

50g

Plain Flour

370g

★ Add color gel, chocolate for the desired color effect

What to do

  • Soften the Butter : Take the butter out of the fridge and let it soften at room temperature
  • Mix Butter and Sugar : Add the caster sugar to the softened butter in batches, mixing well after each addition.
  • Incorporate the Egg : Add the egg to the butter-sugar mixture and mix until fully combined and no longer visible
  • Add Flour : Gradually add the flour to the mixture, sifting it in batches, and mix well.
  • Form and Freeze the Dough : After mixing everything together, divide the dough into 3 portion. Then add chocolate to form the black dough and red color gel for the red dough. Roll the dough into a large, flat sheet, and freeze them.
  • Shape the Dough : Combine the dough as shown below to create the Poké Ball shape
  • Bake the Cookies: Bake the cookies at 120°C (248°F) for 20 minutes. (Bake at low heat to keep the color unchanged)
