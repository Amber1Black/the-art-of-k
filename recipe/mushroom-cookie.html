RECIPE

Mushroom Cookie

These delightful cookies are almost too cute to eat! Shaped like classic Mario mushrooms and stars, they bring a playful twist to any snack time. While they may take a bit of time to prepare, the joy they bring is well worth the effort. Perfect for tea-time treats, these cookies are sure to light up faces, especially for kids who adore their whimsical design.

Category : Cookie

Yield : ~60 pieces

Total Time : 3 hours

Date Created : 04/09/2024

❮ ❯

What you need


Butter

240g

Caster
Sugar

80g


Egg

50g

Plain
Flour

370g


Chocolate

30g

★ Add color gel for the desired color effect

What to Do

  • Soften the Butter : Take the butter out of the fridge and let it soften at room temperature
  • Mix Butter and Sugar : Add the caster sugar to the softened butter in batches, mixing well after each addition.
  • Incorporate the Egg : Add the egg to the butter-sugar mixture and mix until fully combined and no longer visible.
  • Add Flour : Gradually sift the flour into the mixture in batches, mixing well after each addition.
  • Form and Freeze the Dough : After mixing, divide the dough into three portions. Add yellow food coloring to one portion and red food coloring to another. Roll each portion into a large, flat sheet and freeze them.
  • Shape the Dough : Combine the dough as shown below to create the mushroom shape.
  • Bake the Cookies : Bake the cookies at 120°C (248°F) for 20 minutes. Bake at low heat to preserve the color.
  • Final Decoration : Use melted chocolate to add eyes to the cookies.
