Flower Butter Cookie

These charming flower-shaped cookies are the perfect snack for any time of day. With their rich butter flavor and melt-in-your-mouth texture, they provide a deliciously indulgent treat that’s both crispy and tender. Ideal for satisfying your sweet tooth, these cookies are a delightful snack that pairs wonderfully with tea or coffee.

RECIPE

Flower Butter Cookie

These charming flower-shaped cookies are the perfect snack for any time of day. With their rich butter flavor and melt-in-your-mouth texture, they provide a deliciously indulgent treat that’s both crispy and tender. Ideal for satisfying your sweet tooth, these cookies are a delightful snack that pairs wonderfully with tea or coffee.

Category : Cookie

Yield : 52 pieces

Total Time : 2 hours

Date Created : 05/09/2024

❮ ❯

What you need

Butter

245g

Caster Sugar

95g

Egg

50g

Plain Flour

275g

What to Do

  • Soften the Butter : Remove the butter from the fridge and let it sit at room temperature until it softens.
  • Cream the Sugar : Gradually add the sugar to the softened butter, mixing well between each addition until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  • Incorporate the Egg : Add the egg in two separate additions, mixing thoroughly after each addition to ensure it’s fully incorporated.
  • Add the Flour : Gradually add the flour in batches, sifting it to avoid lumps. Mix until the dough is smooth and well combined.
  • Pipe the Batter : Using a 2D piping nozzle, pipe the batter into flower shapes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  • Chill the Batter : Place the piped batter in the refrigerator and freeze for at least 1 hour before baking.
  • Preheat the Oven : Preheat your oven to 240°C (464°F) for 3 minutes.
  • Bake the Cookies : Bake the cookies at 180°C (356°F) for 18 minutes, or until they turn golden brown. Note that oven temperatures can vary, so you may need to adjust the baking time and temperature based on your oven.
