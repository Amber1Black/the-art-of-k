RECIPE

Chocolate Twist Bread

Chocolate twist bread combines the rich, creamy flavor of chocolate (or hazelnut) paste with a soft, fluffy dough to create a truly irresistible treat. We can get creative with different braiding techniques to shape the dough in unique ways, adding a touch of artistry to every bread.

Category : Bread

Yield : 9 pieces

Total Time : 3 hours

Date Created : 30/08/2024

❮ ❯

What you need

Bread
Flour

360g


Sugar

30g


Salt

2g


Yeast

6g


Milk

250g


Butter

25g

★ Chocolate Paste : Add according to preference

What to do

  • Mix all ingredients (except butter) together, and then knead it for 3 minutes at low speed. A rough dough will be formed.
  • Add butter and knead at medium speed for 4 minutes, until a smooth dough is formed.
  • Shape the dough into a round shape, then let it rise in a warm place until it doubles in size. Cover it with a wet cloth to prevent it from drying out.
    [Rising time may vary with different weather conditions. In summer, it may take about 1 hour, while in winter, it may take up to 2 hours.]
  • Divide the dough into 9 portions (approximately 72g each), shape them into round dough, and let them rest for 15 minutes.
  • Form the dough according to the following steps :
  • Let the formed dough proof for at least 30 minutes. It should increase by at least 80% in size (ideally, it should double).
    [Proofing time also depends on the weather. In summer, it may take 30 minutes, while in winter, it could be up to 1 hour.]
  • Add almond flakes on top before baking.
  • Bake for 20 minutes at 180°C (356°F)
    [Ovens vary, so adjust the time and temperature as needed to get the best results.]
  • After removing from the oven, brush the bread with butter to make them shiny.
