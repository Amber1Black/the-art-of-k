RECIPE

Butterfly Palmier

These Butterfly Palmiers are irresistibly crispy and light, making them the perfect snack that you can't stop eating one by one. With a delightful caramelized sugar coating and a delicate crunch, they are a treat for all ages!

Category : Pastry

Yield : 24 pieces

Total Time : 4.5 hours

Date Created : 04/09/2024

❮ ❯

What you need

Plain
Flour

150g

Strong
Flour

50g


Butter

50g

Cold
Water

60g

Caster
Sugar

40g

What to Do

  • Mix the plain flour and strong flour together.
  • Chop the butter into pieces and add it to the flour. Mix the flour and butter together by hand, gently rubbing them until combined.
  • Add water, and continue mixing by hand until a dough forms. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  • Roll the dough into a large sheet, then fold it into thirds. Refrigerate again for 30 minutes. Repeat this step two more times.
  • Roll the dough out into a large sheet again, then sprinkle with sugar. Roll the dough inward from both sides, with one side slightly larger than the other. Freeze for another 20 minutes, pressing the sugar lightly to help it adhere to the dough.
  • Cut the rolled dough into pieces to form butterfly shapes.
  • Sprinkle more sugar on top before baking.
  • Preheat the oven to 240°C (464°F) for 3 minutes. Bake at 160°C (320°F) for 20 minutes. (Every oven is different, so you may need to adjust the time and temperature to suit your oven.)
